About the Tournament

The FIFA World Cup is the premier international football competition contested by the senior men's national teams of the members of the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA).

The championship has been awarded every four years since the inaugural tournament in 1930, except in 1942 and 1946 when it was not held because of the Second World War. It is the most prestigious football tournament in the world, as well as the most widely viewed and followed single sporting event on the planet.
